## Chapters

1. **Time-Independent Perturbation Theory** — Compute first- and second-order energy corrections.
2. **Degenerate Perturbation Theory and Fine Structure** — Diagonalize within a degenerate subspace; get fine structure.
3. **The Variational Principle** — Bound a ground-state energy and simulate the trial-function search.
4. **The WKB Approximation and Tunneling** — Apply WKB to bound states and barrier tunneling.
5. **Time-Dependent Perturbation Theory and Transitions** — Compute transition probabilities; simulate Rabi oscillation.
6. **Radiation, Selection Rules, and Fermi's Golden Rule** — Derive selection rules and a transition rate.
7. **Scattering I: Cross Sections and Partial Waves** — Compute a cross-section by partial-wave analysis.
8. **Scattering II: The Born Approximation** — Apply the Born approximation and simulate the scattered wave.
9. **Atoms in Fields: Zeeman, Stark, and Magnetic Resonance** — Predict level splitting in applied fields.
10. **Periodic Potentials and the Band Structure of Solids** — Derive bands from a periodic potential; simulate the dispersion.
11. **Capstone — Modeling a Real Quantum System** — Model and defend a chosen real system end-to-end.
